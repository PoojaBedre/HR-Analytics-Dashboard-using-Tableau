# HR-Analytics-Dashboard-using-Tableau

**PROJECT NAME**

**HR Analytics:** A Complete Employee Data Analysis Project using Tableau Dashboard.

**ABOUT PROJECT:**
This HR analytics project in Tableau is designed to offer HR managers and business leaders a robust tool for analyzing and monitoring employee data, enabling data-driven decisions on employee retention, development, and recruitment.
The dashboard includes trendlines and target lines to help track progress in reducing attrition. It features charts displaying the distribution of employees and attrition rates by gender, age group, job satisfaction, and education field. Interactive filters and legends are also incorporated, allowing HR managers to drill down into the data for deeper insights.

**DASHBOARD CONTENT**

1st Sheet: Key Performance Indicators (KPI) displaying Employee Count, Attrition Count, Attrition Rate, Active Employees, and Average Age.

2nd Sheet: Attrition by Gender presented as a Lollipop Chart.

3rd Sheet: Department-wise Attrition shown using a Pie Chart.

4th Sheet: Number of Employees by Age Group visualized with a Bar Graph.

5th Sheet: Job Satisfaction Ratings displayed in a Square Chart.

6th Sheet: Attrition by Education Field represented through a Vertical Bar Graph.

**DASHBOARD OVERVIEW**

The HR Analytics Dashboard project, built using Tableau, is designed to offer key insights into human resource management metrics and trends within an organization. Through a variety of visualizations and data analysis techniques, the dashboard helps HR professionals identify patterns and make informed, data-driven decisions. Key visualizations cover crucial HR metrics such as employee turnover rates, headcount, and engagement levels, alongside recruitment data including time-to-fill and cost-per-hire.

The dashboard also tracks employee performance metrics, providing insights into areas like training and development, performance appraisals, and career progression. These insights allow HR professionals to identify areas for improvement and devise strategies to boost employee engagement and productivity.

Interactive features, such as filters and drill-down capabilities, enable users to explore data by department, location, or job level. This functionality helps HR professionals uncover specific patterns and trends for a more detailed view of their workforce data.

Overall, the HR Analytics Dashboard is a powerful tool for HR professionals seeking to leverage Tableau's data visualization capabilities to gain deeper insights and make strategic, data-driven decisions that contribute to organizational success.
